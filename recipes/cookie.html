Cookie Recipe

cookies are simple pastries that melt in your mouth. the most basic is sugar cookies but my personal favorite is the chocolate chip cookie.

ingredients

  • all purpose flour
  • unsalted butter
  • granulated sugar
  • brown sugar
  • vanila extract
  • baking soda
  • salt
  • chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat and Prepare: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper to prevent sticking.
  2. Combine Dry Ingredients:In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
  3. Cream Butter and Sugars:In a large bowl or stand mixer, beat the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar together until the mixture is smooth, creamy, and light in color (about 2 to 4 minutes).
  4. Add Wet Ingredients:Beat in the eggs one at a time, followed by the vanilla extract. Mix until fully combined, scraping down the sides of the bowl as needed.
  5. Mix Wet and Dry:Gradually add the flour mixture to the butter mixture. Mix on low speed until just combined—do not overmix.
  6. Add Chocolate: Fold in the chocolate chips or chunks with a spatula.
  7. Scoop Dough:Scoop 2–3 tablespoons of dough per cookie and place them on the prepared baking sheets, leaving about 2 inches of space between them to allow for spreading.
  8. Bake:Bake for 8–10 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown but the center still looks slightly soft.
  9. Cool:Let the cookies sit on the baking sheet for 5 minutes to settle, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.
