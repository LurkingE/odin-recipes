Chicken Alfredo

This easy Chicken Alfredo recipe includes golden pan-fried seasoned chicken breasts and tender noodles, coated in the most dreamy cream sauce ever. A showstopper meal and its all done in just over 30 minutes!

Ingredients

  • pasta
  • chicken breasts
  • italia seasonings
  • salt
  • pepper
  • olive oil
  • butter
  • cheese
  • garlic powder
  • minced garlic
  • heavy whipping cream

Steps

  1. cook pasta in salted water for 10 minutes
  2. season the chicken with salt and pepper
  3. warm olive oil over medium heat in a nonstick skillet
  4. once shimmering add chicken and leave for 5 to 7 minuted until golden at the buttom
  5. flip it over and add 1 tablespoon of butter tot he pan
  6. let it cook for another 5 to 7 until the chicken is golden all around
  7. whisk the heavy cream and some butter
  8. put in the same pan as the chicken and add minced garlic, garlic powder, italian seasoning, salt, pepper and whisk until combined smoothly
  9. cook this for about 3 minutes
  10. stir in the cheese until it melts then add in the pasta and youre done.
